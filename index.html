Glen Oak Elementary School welcomes you for a fun tournament that has something for everyone! USCF Membership is encouraged and required for the rated section. ($24 for a year through their site.)

Time controls: Both sections will be ?, d?. This will be a five round (6 if there’s time) Swiss System tournament. All players will play all rounds and no one gets eliminated.
Sections: rated. Note: USCF membership is mandatory for those playing in the rated section. Thinking of joining USCF? Now is a great time to sign up and play in a fun, low-key setting. Please log on to USCF.org or purchase a membership in the event registration and the tournament director will assist in setting up a youth USCF membership.
Entry fees: $10.00 per student. Register here. Concessions will also be available. “Glen Oak Meals” will be available for $5.00 (includes a slice of pizza, drink, bag of chips and cookie).
Check-in: Players can check in as early as 6:00pm. I would encourage all families to be checked in by 6:30pm. Please purchase a USCF membership on the registration form, if applicable. Announcements will be made at 6:45 and play will begin at 7:00. The goal is to have everything wrapped up by 9:00.

Space is limited! The event will be capped at 80 participants! Please register early! No registrations will be accepted after Wednesday, January 29, 2025 at 8:00 p.m.
Disclaimer: If school is cancelled, the event will be rescheduled.
